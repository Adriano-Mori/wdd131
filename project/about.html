◇◈ About This Page ◈◇

Discover Peru is student project designed to share more facts about this Andean nation. This page serves as a visual and informational site, showcasing Peru's natural beauty, deep cultural richness, and significant historical landmarks. The shows key destinations, from the ancient wonders of Machu Picchu to the vibrant geological formations of Vinicunca, the stunning desert oasis of Huacachina,the breathtaking depths of the Colca Canyon, and many more. All of this while providing interesting facts and essential visitor information.

◇◈ The Mission ◈◇

Our main mission is to provide a simple, attractive, and informative platform for anyone who wants to learn about this amazing country. We aim to make the wonders of Peru accessible, whether you are interested in its tall Andean mountains, the secrets of its coast and desert, or the rich story of its history. Most importantly, we want to spark real curiosity, promote future travels, and encourage a deep appreciation for Peru's unique diversity.

◇ Who Created This and Why? ◇

This site was created as project for a web development course. During the whole process all the content, design choices, and page layouts were made from scratch using basic web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This site showcases how the technical skills learnt in this course were applied to a topic of cultural matter.
