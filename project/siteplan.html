(Site Plan) Discover Peru

-> Site Name

Discover Peru

This name represents a website focused on exploring many aspects of Peru. Clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give visitors helpful information about Peru. It will show major attractions, cultural stuff, activities, and more. The site has an objective of helping users to learn about what Peru offers to the world.

Scenarios

  • “What are the must see places in Peru?” - A visitor wants to know the best tourist attractions.
  • “When is the best time of year to visit Peru?” - A traveler wants practical planning details.

Color Schema

Main Colors:

  • #8B0000 - Deep Red Used for headings, borders, and accents.
  • #F4A300 - Gold Used for highlights, buttons, icons, and section accents.
  • #FFFFFF - White Used for clean background areas.
  • #000000 - Black Used for regular text.

Typography

  • Bree seriff - Used for headings
  • ABeeZee - Used for headings (h2)
  • Raleway - Used for paragraphs and body text
