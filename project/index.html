◇ Machu Picchu - Cuzco ◇

Machu Picchu is Peru's most famous landmark and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. A stronghold built by the Inca civilization in the 15th century, it stands high in the Andes Mountains and offers amazing views of green valleys and stone terraces. Visitors can explore its temples, stairways, and plazas while learning about the ancient Inca culture. Today, Machu Picchu is a symbol of Peru's history and a destination for travelers from around the world must see.
