Perú

Official Flag of Perú

Perú is a country on the western coast of South America. It is bordered by Ecuador and Colombia to the north, Brazil to the east, Bolivia to the southeast, Chile to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. The country is known for its diverse geography, having different landscapes from the Andes Mountains to the Amazon rainforest and deserts. Peru is also famous for its cuisine, music, and traditions, and a major tourist destination for its ancient sites. Machu Picchu, the iconic Inca monument located in the city of Cuzco, is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
